Hi, we're Cooking Club.

We at Cooking Club understand that cooking can be a bit overwhelming at the beginning, which makes many people either lose interest or motivation, which we believe is a shame! That is why we opened locations around Austria to offer guidance and a community and lessen both fear and requirements for young people. Our goal is to spread the art of cooking and help young people, both life more independently and more healthy!
Upon signing up we offer members 24/7 support, a place to communicate with other members which include but are not limited to a slack and much more!
